About Us

FAMILY OWNED AND OPERATED SINCE 2008

Camp Rd Smash Repairs has spent almost two decades turning damaged cars back into ones owners are proud to drive — one honest quote at a time.

Our Story

Originally O&V Complete Auto Repairs, this automotive repair shop opened with a single goal: give local drivers a place they could trust completely with their car, whether they were paying out of pocket or going through insurance. Our owner Oscar provides the upmost care to every car— ensuring they all leave looking the way it should have before the damage happened.

Now operating as Camp Rd Smash Repairs, we’ve kept the same trusted care while raising our standards to deliver the professional experience every customer should expect.
